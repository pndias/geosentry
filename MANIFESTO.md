# 📜 GeoSentry Manifesto: Intelligence for Life

GeoSentry is not a market tool or a war toy for elites. It is a **Data Shield** built by and for the people of the Global South. This manifesto defines the non-negotiable principles that govern every line of code and every analysis generated by this platform.

## 1. Primacy of Human Life
GeoSentry's success is not measured by profit or cold statistical accuracy, but by its ability to provide information that saves lives. In any conflict, our analysis prioritizes the impact on civilians, workers, and the vulnerable.

## 2. Global South Perspective
We reject the Eurocentric or North American worldview as the sole truth. Our AI agents and collaborators seek to understand how geopolitical movements affect the sovereignty and well-being of the peoples of Latin America, Africa, Asia, and Oceania.

## 3. Plurality and Working-Class Honesty
This project is maintained by workers. We accept and encourage diversity of thought and political views, as long as they are grounded in honesty and commitment to social justice. GeoSentry is a space of convergence for those who work, not for those who exploit.

## 4. Technology as an Instrument of Liberation
Artificial Intelligence must serve to reduce information asymmetry. While elites use data to control, we use data to liberate, alert, and prepare.

## 5. Transparency and Open Source
We believe in collective intelligence. GeoSentry must remain accessible, allowing local communities to adapt the tool to their specific realities of survival and resistance.
